Surf Guiding

Explore Morocco’s best breaks with expert local guides, personalized spot selection, and a flexible surf schedule built around swell and tide.

“When we succeed, the credit goes to our surf guide. He is always by your side.” - Zaya Surf Morocco -

Overview

Surf Guiding is for intermediate to advanced surfers who want to discover Morocco’s best waves with an experienced local guide. Every day is adapted to swell, tides, and your surf level.

We organize small groups, flexible sessions, and smooth logistics so you can focus on the lineup and the feeling of surfing new spots.

Typical Day

Every day is tailored to the best conditions. Timing can shift based on swell, wind, and tide.

  • 07:30 Breakfast with Moroccan flavors
  • 09:00 Surf briefing + choose the best spot
  • 10:00 Guided surf session #1
  • 13:00 Beach lunch + relax
  • 15:30 Guided surf session #2
  • 19:30 Moroccan dinner at the house
