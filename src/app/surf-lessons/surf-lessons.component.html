Surf Lessons

Overview

Surf Lessons are tailored for beginners and intermediate surfers who want to build strong fundamentals, improve technique, and enjoy the fun of surf house life.

Our instructors guide every session, help you choose the right waves, and keep the vibe relaxed and encouraging from sunrise to sunset.

Typical Day

Timing shifts based on the tide and swell to make sure you surf the safest, easiest waves.

  • 07:30 Breakfast at the surf house
  • 09:00 Transfer to the beach + warm-up
  • 10:00 Morning lesson with instructors
  • 13:00 Beach lunch + relax
  • 15:30 Free surf session + coaching tips
  • 19:30 Moroccan dinner with the group
