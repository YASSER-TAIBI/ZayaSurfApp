The Moon — light, calm, and effortless comfort

The Moon is our most calming hideaway, designed for guests who want soft textures, gentle lighting, and space to breathe after long surf sessions. The room blends natural wood, woven textiles, and airy windows that invite the Atlantic breeze. Every corner is quiet and intentional, from the reading nook to the bed dressed in crisp cotton.

Wake up to sunlit mornings, enjoy a slow coffee on the terrace, and return at sunset to a room that feels instantly grounding. Whether you are traveling as a couple or with a surf buddy, The Moon offers privacy, peaceful rest, and the kind of slow living that makes each day feel longer.
